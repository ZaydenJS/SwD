Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services and process

Do you provide ongoing maintenance?

+

Yes! We offer ongoing maintenance packages that include security updates, content updates, performance monitoring, and technical support. Our maintenance plans can be customized to your needs.

How long does it take to build a website?

+

The timeline depends on the complexity of your project. Full custom websites with mobile and PC optimization take anywhere from 4-14 days. While ecommerce websites and full booking system websites may take anywhere from 1-3 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

What's included in your web design service?

+

Every build is custom‑engineered for your brand: responsive design, high‑performance development, CMS setup, and $25 a month of post‑launch support. We handle everything end‑to‑end, including secure hosting recommendations and domain setup. No handoff.

Can you help with SEO and digital marketing?

+

Absolutely! We offer technical full‑backend SEO as an optional add‑on and can extend with advanced SEO services, content strategy, and conversion optimization as needed.

What if I need changes after the website is live?

+

We include $25 a month with every project for minor changes and updates. After that, choose a maintenance plan or request larger updates via our streamlined change request process.
