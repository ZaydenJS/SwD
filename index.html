Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our engineering process

How long does a typical project take?

+

Project timelines vary based on complexity, but most custom websites take 4-14 days from discovery to launch. We provide detailed timelines during our initial consultation and keep you updated throughout the process. For large or highly custom builds, timelines may extend beyond this range, we'll confirm during discovery.

Do you use templates or build everything custom?

+

We build 100% custom solutions from scratch. No templates, no themes, no shortcuts. Every line of code is engineered specifically for your brand and business goals.

What's included in your ongoing support?

+

Our support includes 24/7 monitoring, security updates, performance optimization, content updates, and technical support. We handle everything end‑to‑end — no handoff.

How do you ensure fast loading times?

+

We use advanced optimization techniques including code splitting, image optimization, CDN deployment, and performance monitoring. Our mobile experiences are optimized to achieve a sub‑3s Speed Index.

Can you help with SEO and digital marketing?

+

Absolutely! We offer technical full‑backend SEO as an optional add‑on, and can extend with advanced SEO services, content strategy, and conversion optimization as needed.

What if I need changes after the site is live?

+

We include 3 months of post‑launch support with every project. After that, minor updates are available through our maintenance plans starting at $50/month, and major changes are handled through our streamlined change request process.

Do you work with businesses outside your local area?

+

Yes! We work with clients globally. Our remote-first approach means we can deliver exceptional results regardless of location. We use modern collaboration tools to stay connected throughout the project.

How do you determine pricing for custom projects?

+

We believe every project is unique, so we don't use rigid packages. Instead, we offer transparent, custom pricing based on your specific requirements. After our consultation call, we provide a detailed proposal with fixed costs, timeline, and payment milestones, no hidden fees, no surprises.
